LEVEL CREATION GUIDE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Level Creation is divided into placing the Ground and the Objects, as well as the Name determination.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Determination should follow the example

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Items are placed with indication numbers seperated by space.

For Ground files:
0 = Water
1 = Sand
2 = Gras
3 = Box in water
4 = WaterHole
5 = SandHole
6 = SandHole with Bean
7 = Spring

For Objects files:
0 = None
//  1 = Pushy
2 = House
3 = Box
4 = Stone
5 = Palm left
6 = Palm right
7 = Seastar
8 = Bottle
9 = Bean
10 = Bottle water
